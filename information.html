Some general things to note about how to use this website.

About the papers

When a paper is unavailable, it is marked with QP or Memo in red.

Some papers are marked N/A to indicate that the exam was postponed due to COVID.

We have sorted out all the papers to be in the right order and folder, but dates of the file names may sometimes be misleading, open the paper and see cover page for when the paper was written.

We value your feedback

If you encounter a paper that doesn't load or it's been posted in the wrong place, please let us know and we'll fix it right away.

How to download a paper

A technology-first approach to payments

Seamless experience

Downloading a paper

  • 1
    Click on the download icon

    You will be directed to a safe Payfast payment page.

  • 2
    Enter your details

    1. See if the description matches the paper you want to download.

    2. Enter your details and follow the prompts specifc to your bank and the payment method you have chosen.

  • 3
    Download Page

    At this final page, the paper should automatically download.

    If the paper opens in your browser (i.e for all papers from Nov 2022), you still need to click on options to download it.

    Only close the page when you are sure the paper has downloaded.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do i view a paper for free?
Older papers are available for viewing online, you just won't be able to download them unless you purchase.

Click on the word QP or Memo to view
How does Bulk Purchasing work?
If you're looking to download more than 12 papers at once, we offer bulk purchasing.

Simply send us an email or text to request all the papers you'd like and we'll quote you, then email them to you within a few minutes.

All Bulk Purchases come with a 30% discount.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes! If you are not happy with the papers you've received, you can request for a refund
Do you keep NCV past papers?
We don't keep NCV papers at the moment. This may or may not change in the near future.
